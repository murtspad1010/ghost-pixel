How it works

This tool uses a method called LSB (Least Significant Bit) steganography. Every pixel in your image consists of Red, Green, and Blue values (0-255). We convert your text into binary and replace the very last bit of each color channel with your message bits. Because the change is only 1/255th of the color value, the difference is invisible to the human eye.
